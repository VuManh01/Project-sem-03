Strawberry Ice Cream Recipe

  • Step 1:Prepare Strawberries
  • Step 2:Mix Wet
  • Step 3:Combine
  • Step 4:Churn

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced

Tools

  • Ice cream maker (if available)
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk

Instructions

Step 1:Prepare Strawberries: In a bowl, mash the strawberries with a fork or potato masher. Add 1/4 cup of sugar to the strawberries and let them sit for 10-15 minutes to release their juice, then blend them until smooth.

Step 2:Mix Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, vanilla extract, and the remaining 1/2 cup of sugar until the sugar dissolves completely.

Step 3:Combine: Mix the pureed strawberries into the cream mixture, stirring well to combine.

Step 4:Churn & Freeze: Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions (about 20-25 minutes). Then transfer the churned ice cream into an airtight container and freeze for 3-4 hours until firm.
