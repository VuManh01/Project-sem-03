Banana Recipe

  • Step 1:Prepare
  • Step 2:Blend
  • Step 3:Mix
  • Step 4:Combine

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 4 ripe bananas

Tools

  • Ice cream maker (if available)
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk

Instructions

Step 1:Prepare the Bananas: Peel the bananas and slice them into small pieces. Place the banana slices on a baking sheet and freeze them for at least 2 hours or until fully frozen.

Step 2:Blend the Bananas: Once the banana slices are frozen, blend them in a food processor or blender until smooth and creamy. It may take a few minutes, and you may need to scrape down the sides of the blender.

Step 3:Mix Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, mix together the heavy cream, whole milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

Step 4:Combine & Freeze: Stir the banana puree into the cream mixture. Then, transfer the mixture into an airtight container and freeze for 3-4 hours until firm.
