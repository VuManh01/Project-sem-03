Coffee Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 teaspoon finely ground coffee (press grinds through a fine mesh sieve)

Tools

  • Ice cream maker (if available)
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk

Instructions

Step 1:Heat the milk, sugar, whole coffee beans, salt, and 1/2 cup of the cream in a medium saucepan until it is quite warm and steamy, but not boiling. Once the mixture is warm, cover, remove from the heat, and let steep at room temperature for 1 hour.

Step 2:Pour the remaining 1 cup of cream into a medium metal bowl and set it in a large bowl full of ice. Set a mesh strainer on top of the bowls. Set aside.

Step 3:Reheat the milk and coffee mixture on medium heat until it's hot and steamy again (not boiling!)

Step 4:Chill the mixture thoroughly in the refrigerator, then freeze it in your ice cream maker according to the manufacturer's instructions.
