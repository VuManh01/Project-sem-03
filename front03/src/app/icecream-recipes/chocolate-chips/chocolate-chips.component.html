Choco Chips Recipe

  • Step 1:Preheat & Cream
  • Step 2: Combine Dry Ingredients
  • Step 3: Add Chocolate Chips
  • Step 4: Bake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Tools

  • Ice cream maker (if available)
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk

Instructions

Step 1:Preheat & Cream: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.

Step 2:Combine Dry Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the creamed butter mixture, mixing until combined.

Step 3:Add Chocolate Chips: Stir in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

Step 4: Bake: Drop tablespoon-sized scoops of dough onto a baking sheet, spaced about 2 inches apart. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. Let them cool on the sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
