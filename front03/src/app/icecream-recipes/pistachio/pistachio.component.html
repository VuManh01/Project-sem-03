Pistachio Ice Cream Recipe

  • Step 1:Add
  • Step 2:Pour
  • Step 3:Stir
  • Step 4:Enjoy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Whole shelled pistachios

Tools

  • Ice cream maker (if available)
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk

Instructions

Step 1:Add ½ cup pistachios and ¾ cup sugar to a food processor and process until finely chopped and blended.

Step 2:Pour the custard through a fine-mesh sieve set over a bowl; discard what’s left in the strainer. Cover mixture and refrigerate for several hours or overnight, until completely chilled. (Depending on the your ice cream maker, you may need to freeze the bowl of the ice cream maker overnight as well).

Step 3:Stir in remaining ½ cup pistachios. Transfer the ice cream to a container, cover and freeze until firm, at least 3 hours.

Step 4:Enjoy!
