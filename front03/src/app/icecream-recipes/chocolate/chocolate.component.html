Choco Ice Cream Recipe

  • Step 1: Mix Ingredients
  • Step 2: Add Vanilla
  • Step 3: Melt
  • Step 4: Remove from heat
  • Step 5: Churn
  • Step 6: Freeze
  • Step 7: Enjoy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (optional, for extra richness)

Tools

  • Ice cream maker (if available)
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk

Instructions

Step 1: Mix dry ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.

Step 2:Heat milk and cream: In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the whole milk and heavy cream. Heat until it's warm but not boiling.

Step 3: Melt the chocolate: If using chocolate chips, add them to the saucepan. Stir until the chocolate is melted and fully incorporated.

Step 4: Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract. Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate it for at least 2 hours (or overnight) to chill thoroughly.

Step 5: Churn the ice cream: Once the mixture is chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions (usually about 20-25 minutes).

Step 6: Freeze: Transfer the churned ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for about 3-4 hours to harden.

Step 7: Serve: Scoop and enjoy your homemade chocolate ice cream!
