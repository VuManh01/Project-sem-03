Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

  • Step 1: Mix Ingredients
  • Step 2: Add Vanilla
  • Step 3: Freeze
  • Step 4: Enjoy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Tools

  • Ice cream maker (if available)
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Whisk

Instructions

Step 1: Combine heavy cream, milk, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Whisk until the sugar is dissolved.

Step 2: Add vanilla extract to the mixture and stir well.

Step 3: Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and follow the machine's instructions. If you don't have an ice cream maker, place the mixture in the freezer and stir every 30 minutes until smooth and creamy.

Step 4: Once ready, scoop the ice cream into bowls or containers and enjoy!
